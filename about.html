Phantasm Comics was founded by Dan Richards and Selena McDonald in December of 2020. With a love for all things spooky and a passion for creative arts, they launched a comic book publisher with a clear vision: to entertain readers with unique stores. They have a talented team of writers and artists who share their passion and as a result have caught on in the indie comics scene. In addition, Phantasm appeals to a wide demographic ranging from early readers to young adults. Titles can be purchased from your favorite comic or book store or can be purchased online.
